About

Where Science
Meets Precision

Dr. Garima Asthana is a specialist periodontist and implantologist whose career spans clinical excellence, cutting-edge research, and academic leadership. Currently serving as Senior Lecturer and Deputy Research Director at Manav Rachna Dental College, she is also a PhD candidate exploring the frontier of regenerative periodontics.

Her research focuses on the oral-systemic interface — particularly the bidirectional relationship between periodontal disease, diabetes mellitus, and obesity — earning her the International Best Researcher Award at the International Congress for Research Excellence 2024.
